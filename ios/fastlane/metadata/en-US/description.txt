family for WWDC is a simple app that allows you to share your physical location for other attendees of WWDC. 

Features:
- Real-time location sharing with fellow attendees
- Tap marker to open the Twitter profile to easily tweet or DM to a specific person
- Automatic fetching of latest profile pictures from Twitter
- Toggle your location sharing with one easy tap, allows you to easily stop sharing when you're heading to the hotel
- See all the WWDC Parties right on the map

Please note that this app was created by Felix Krause - It is no way affiliated with Apple Inc.
